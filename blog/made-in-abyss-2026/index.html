THE DARKNESS RETURNS: MADE IN ABYSS MOVIE TRILOGY ANNOUNCED FOR 2026

PUBLISHED: JUNE 1, 2026 · 6 MIN READ · BY DREAMING ANIME VANGUARD

One of anime's most critically acclaimed and visceral franchises is officially making its high-fidelity return. As of this morning, the newsroom has confirmed that Made in Abyss: The Awakening Mystery, a new movie series, is scheduled for a staggered release starting in early 2026.

This trilogy marks a significant shift in the series' production, aiming for a theatrical experience that matches the sheer environmental scale of the Abyss itself. For the uninitiated, Made in Abyss has always been a masterclass in tonal juxtaposition—pairing cute, high-fidelity character designs with some of the most harrowing and deep psychological storytelling in the medium.

The first part, titled The Awakening Mystery, will dive deeper into the mysteries of the 7th Layer, picking up the narrative threads left dangling after the 2022 season. Fans can expect a massive leap in animation quality as the studio moves to a full theatrical budget to render the bio-luminescent flora and terrifying fauna of the deeper layers.

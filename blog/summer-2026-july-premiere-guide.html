The heat is rising, but the anime slate for July 2026 is even hotter. As we transition into the heart of the Summer season, Crunchyroll and Netflix have unleashed their heavy hitters. From long-awaited sequels to the debut of the next "Big Three" contender, here is everything you need to track this month.

THE HEADLINER: BLACK TORCH

Premiering July 5, 2026. After months of high-fidelity teasers, Tsuyoshi Takaki's shinobi-supernatural epic finally hits the screen. Studio Pierrot has promised "movie-quality" animation for every episode of the first cour. If the rumors of the leaked Episode 1 animation are true, we are looking at the new gold standard for action choreography.

RETURNING GIANTS

Bocchi the Rock! Season 2: The Kessoku Band is back. Expect even more experimental animation styles and social anxiety-fueled bangers. The first episode drops July 10, and the hype in the Shibuya district is already reaching a fever pitch.

The Elusive Samurai Season 2: Tokiyuki's quest for survival continues. CloverWorks is doubling down on the vibrant, historically-rich aesthetics that made Season 1 a sleeper hit.

THE WILD CARD: KAIJU GIRL CARAMELISE

For those looking for something different, this adaptation is set to be the rom-com hit of the summer. When a girl turns into a giant monster when she gets embarrassed? It's the "Gap Moe" event of the year, and the first reviews out of the Tokyo screening are calling it a "visual masterpiece of cuteness and chaos."

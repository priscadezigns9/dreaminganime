The Los Angeles Convention Center has officially transformed into the epicenter of the anime multiverse. Anime Expo (AX) 2026 has kicked off, and the Day One signals are already redefining our expectations for the next two years of animation.

THE BIG REVEAL: KAGURABACHI ANIME

It’s official. Takeru Hokazono’s manga phenomenon, Kagurabachi, is getting an anime adaptation. The announcement trailer, which debuted during the special Shonen Jump panel, confirmed that the series will be handled by a high-fidelity studio collaboration aimed at capturing the manga’s unique cinematic ink-style. The "Chihiro" hype is real, and the katana-action genre just found its next king.

SEQUEL SURGES

The Apothecary Diaries Season 3: Maomao returns. The announcement panel confirmed that production for Season 3 is well underway, with a target release window for late 2026. Fans were treated to a brief teaser showing Maomao investigating a new mystery within the Outer Court.

Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2: Even years later, the legend lives on. A new trailer for the final DLC content was released today, showcasing high-fidelity combat updates and a narrative bridge to the next major Dragon Ball project.

PRODUCT SPOTLIGHT: HUNTER X HUNTER VOL. 39

For the collectors, today marks the official release of Hunter x Hunter Volume 39 in Japan. The volume includes chapters 401-410, and international fans are already scrambling for imports to see Yoshihiro Togashi's latest masterwork in physical form.
